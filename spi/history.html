Company History

Established in 1995, Strategic Partners is a leader in the manufacturing and marketing of medical uniforms, medical footwear and school uniforms with products sold through uniform shops, specialty catalogs and mass-market retailers. Our corporate name, Strategic Partners, Inc., reflects our philosophy of developing strong and lasting partnerships with our customers, employees, vendors and licensing associates.

Our core purpose has always been to "Make customers look and feel good." We accomplish this by designing and producing high quality, fashion-inspired, comfortable uniforms and footwear.

We operate our businesses under a variety of brands, including Cherokee®, Dickies®, HeartSoul® and Code HappyTM. We are also licensees for many additional well-known brands, such as Disney®, Hello Kitty®, Marvel® and numerous others.

Strategic Partners has approximately 500 employees, primarily in California and Texas. Our corporate offices and design studio are located in Chatsworth, California, and our distribution is carried out in our state-of-the-art distribution center in Dallas, Texas.
