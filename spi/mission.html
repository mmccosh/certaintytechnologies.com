About Us

Established in 1995 and based in Chatsworth, California, Strategic Partners (SPI) is the industry leader in the design, manufacture and distribution of high-quality, fashion-inspired medical apparel, medical footwear and school uniforms.

About Us

Medical Apparel: SPI designs, manufactures and distributes under the Cherokee, Dickies, HeartSoul, Code Happy and Disney brands.

Medical Footwear: SPI manufactures and/or distributes medical footwear under the Cherokee, Dickies, Asics and K-Swiss brands, among many others.

School Uniforms: SPI manufactures and distributes Classroom brand school uniforms.

The company supports nurses and other healthcare professionals through Scrubs Magazine, the leading lifestyle media brand for nurses, its website, scrubsmag.com and ScrubsBeat, a video channel for nurses. Strategic Partners also engages in numerous community outreach and philanthropic activities.

Our Mission Statement

As the leader in the design, manufacturing and marketing of medical uniforms, medical footwear and school uniforms, SPI has a threefold mission:

1. Create a warmer, friendlier and healthier medical environment through unique, state-of-the-art products and technologies.

2. Design, create and market uniforms that provide outstanding value, uncompromising quality and industry-leading style in ethically produced and competitively priced apparel.

3. Create quality publications and digital media that inform, educate and celebrate healthcare professionals.

In order to fulfill our mission, we continuously focus on:

  • Brand and product excellence
  • Unparalleled service
  • Retail partners
  • Healthcare workers
  • Sustainability
